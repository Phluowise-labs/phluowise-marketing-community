Quick Actions

Team Management

Easily manage your team members, track their performance, and handle payments all in one place.

Adding Team Members

  1. Click on "Add Member" in the Team Management section
  2. Enter the member's email and assign a role
  3. Set their payment distribution percentage
  4. Click "Send Invitation"

Payment Distribution

  • Set individual percentages for each team member
  • Automatically calculate payouts based on performance
  • Track payment history and generate reports

Transactions

Track all your transactions, filter by date, status, or amount, and export records for accounting.

Leaderboard

Compete with other teams and track your ranking based on performance metrics.

Profile Settings

Update your personal information, change your password, and manage notification preferences.
